The **MyCitadel Hall of Fame** is not a formality.

The Citadel remembers. The Citadel honors. The Citadel endures — because of them.

### 📋 Responsible Disclosure Policy — Summary

Until our formal bug bounty programs launch, we operate a **responsible disclosure policy**:

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Report To** | `security@mycitadel.io` |
| **Response SLA** | 48 hours acknowledgement, 7 days triage |
| **Safe Harbor** | Good-faith researchers will not face legal action |
| **No Destructive Testing** | Do not delete, modify, or exfiltrate user data |
| **No Social Engineering** | Do not target MyCitadel staff or Citizens |
| **Coordinated Disclosure** | Allow us 90 days to patch before public disclosure |
| **Recognition** | All valid reports earn a place in this Hall of Fame |

<br/>

> ⚠️ **MyCitadel extends full Safe Harbor protection to all good-faith security researchers** operating within the scope of our disclosure policy. We will never pursue legal action against researchers who follow responsible disclosure guidelines. The Citadel respects those who protect it.
