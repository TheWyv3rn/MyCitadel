🔍 Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability within the MyCitadel infrastructure, we ask that you follow our Responsible Disclosure Protocol.

DO NOT open a public Issue on GitHub for security vulnerabilities. This exposes our Citizens to potential exploitation before a patch can be deployed.

Authorized Reporting Channels

To ensure your report is handled with the appropriate level of encryption and priority, please utilize one of our sanctioned platforms:

Official Platforms: HackerOne | BugCrowd | Intigriti | YesWeHack

Direct Encrypted Link: security@mycitadel.lol (Please use PGP for sensitive findings)

⚡ What to Expect (The Auditor's Handshake)

When you submit a report through a sanctioned channel, the Sovereign Genesis Sentinel triggers the following workflow:

Initial Triage: You will receive a human-verified response within 24 hours.

Validation: Our team will attempt to reproduce the finding in a sandbox environment.

Status Updates: You will receive a progress report every 48 hours until the vulnerability is resolved.

The Patch Window: We aim to deploy critical security patches within 72 hours of verification.

Recognition: Once the patch is live, you will be offered a permanent spot in our Hall of Fame and your node hash will be included in our next weekly Friday Watchman report.

Disclosure Policy

We follow a 90-day standard disclosure window. We ask that you do not share details of the vulnerability publicly until the patch has been confirmed and deployed to all active nodes.

📜 Ethical Research Mandate

To remain under the protection of our "Safe Harbor" policy:

No Data Exfiltration: Accessing Citizen data beyond what is needed to prove the vulnerability is strictly prohibited.

No Service Disruption: Do not attempt DoS/DDoS or any action that degrades the Citadel's availability for its Citizens.

Near Zero-Knowledge Integrity: Respect the architecture. Do not attempt to bypass encryption in a way that targets a specific individual's privacy.
